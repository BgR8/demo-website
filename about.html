About

I'm a historian and started exploring the world of coding (Python and Ruby on Rails) since 2012. I worked as a trainee Rails developer and am currently encouraging young women to get into tech at VHTO. I'm spending a large part of my time (co-)organising PyLadies Amsterdam and Rails Girls Amsterdam, reviewing coding tutorials at Codepancake and I am Dutch Ambassador for the European Code Week.

If you'd like to follow me on Twitter, my username is @lieke2208 .
